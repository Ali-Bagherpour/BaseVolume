## About the Project

BaseVolume is a web-based music discovery application that enables users to explore a wide array of music content. It addresses the challenge of finding new music by providing curated trending hits, organized genre browsing, and personalized listening history tracking. Built with a focus on simplicity and accessibility, the platform supports cross-device synchronization for history and offers intuitive playback controls. Whether you're looking for the latest pop trends or classic jazz vibes, BaseVolume makes music exploration engaging and effortless.

The project is originally presented in Persian but has been translated and synthesized into English for broader accessibility.

## Key Features

- **Trending Hits Discovery**: Highlight emerging music tracks with options to listen immediately.
- **Genre Browsing**: Explore a comprehensive list of genres including Pop, Rock, Hip Hop, Jazz, Electronic, R&B, Classical, Metal, Lo-Fi, Country, Reggae, and Blues, each with concise descriptions.
- **Listening History Management**: View and clear your recently played songs across all devices.
